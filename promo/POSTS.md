## Thread (one card per tweet)

1. (attach `out/card-hero.png`)
   > Remote interviews deserve better than "can you hear me?"
   > Tributary is a remote recording studio that runs on YOUR server.
   > Here's how it works 🧵

2. (attach `out/card-local.png`)
   > The core trick: the live call and the recording are completely separate.
   > Each guest's browser records their own camera + mic at full quality and
   > uploads chunks in the background. Bad wifi ruins the call, never the take.

3. (attach `out/card-tracks.png`)
   > When you stop recording, ffmpeg assembles per-guest MP4 + 48kHz WAV +
   > mixed exports — offsets aligned automatically. One click gets you a
   > Premiere/FCP timeline with everything synced.

4. (attach `out/card-editor.png`)
   > Whisper transcribes every track with speaker labels. Select the filler
   > words in the transcript, hit cut — the video follows the text. Export
   > 9:16 clips with burned-in captions for Shorts/Reels.

5. (attach `out/card-agents.png`)
   > And it's AI-agent native: the whole post-production surface is a JSON
   > CLI. Point Claude Code (or any agent) at your episode and ask for
   > highlight clips. No LLM ships in the box — bring your own.

6. (no image, close it out)
   > Self-host it tonight: Node + ffmpeg is all you need.
   > MIT licensed. Stars appreciated ⭐
   > github.com/Realmullens/tributary
